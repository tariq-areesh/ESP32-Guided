Pinout Reference

Pin groups and quick notes

  • Power: 3V3 gives 3.3 V. VIN takes raw supply. GND is ground.
  • Enable: EN resets or enables the chip.
  • Input only: GPIO34, 35, 36, 39.
  • ADC1 vs ADC2: ADC1 on GPIO32-39. ADC2 is limited while Wi-Fi is active.
  • DAC: GPIO25 and GPIO26.
  • Touch: GPIO0, 2, 4, 12-15, 27, 32, 33.
  • I2C default: SDA=GPIO21, SCL=GPIO22.
  • SPI default: MOSI=GPIO23, MISO=GPIO19, SCK=GPIO18, CS=GPIO5.
  • UART0: TX0=GPIO1, RX0=GPIO3. UART2: TX2=GPIO17, RX2=GPIO16.
  • Strapping: GPIO0, 2, 5, 12, 15 affect boot.
  • Reserved: GPIO6-11 are flash pins.
